About Us

The VIT University shuttle transport facility is a convenient and efficient way for students to get around campus. We provide transportation to and from all campus buildings and locations, and we are committed to providing a safe and comfortable ride for everyone.

Services

We offer a variety of transportation options to suit your needs. From the comfort of your own dorm room to the lavish dining hall, we have a shuttle that will take you where you need to go.

If you need to get to a specific location, we can arrange for a shuttle to be transported to your desired location. We also have a variety of on-campus transportation options, such as buses and trains, that you can use to get around campus.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about the shuttle transport facility, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach us by phone at (080) 2345-6789 or by email at shuttle@vituniversity.ac.in.
